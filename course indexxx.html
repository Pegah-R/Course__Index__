At the very beginning, I struggled with coming up with ideas or designs for my website, but I knew I wanted to have monochrome colors, specifically purple. At the same time, having a colorful website as well. My very first layouts were simple with dark purple text and light purple background. To make my website colorful I decided to have each page in different colors, with the elements being different tones of that color. This then lead to the first page having a rainbow going from the top to the bottom, leading and creating a timeline-like design, showing the oldest to the newest assignment. The process started with the rainbow and the clouds being made in photoshop to be added to the website.

The overall design and the first page or the main page of my website tell a lot about my personality and who I am, which was my goal.

The layout of the pages stays pretty consistent and with the development of one of the pages, The rest was modeled after. Figuring out the layout of that one page and being consistent with it was very difficult. The first page that was built was the assignment 2 page, and by then I was still figuring out how to add things to the website or what things to have. Adding links to a text and formatting the link, was very difficult to figure out, with me running into many problems and issues. This feature, however, was very beneficial to my design, for example, the logo at the top leading to the first page.
